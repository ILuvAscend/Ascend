            /p (all) - play
            /s (all) - stop

            /safe - TOGGLE all visible 'hax' and hide the program. (hasnt been updated in a while, possibly doesnt hide everything now)

/save "template" - Create/Save settings as a template.
/load (all) "template" - Load a template.

/fast (all) - Set walk delay to 160 ms.
            /med (all) - Set walk delay to 260 ms.
            /slow (all) - Set walk delay to 410 ms.
            /stop (all) - Stop walking/following.
/follow (all) "name" ("#") - Follow someone, set distance.
/walk (all) "destination" - Walk to an area on the list.
(see bottom of .txt for full list)

/g "name"or(alts)or(friends) - invite someone into your group.
/fg "name"or(alts)or(friends) - force yourself into an existing group.

/calc on - Turn on calculator (starts all 3 if none checked).
            /calc off - Turn off calculator.
            /calc reset - Reset calculator data.
            /calc - Request calculator data.

            LEGIT BANKING ETC. (use at npc)
